Our Story

Rooted in Tradition,
Served with Love

Hotel Sri Vaari has been a cherished part of Saidapet's culinary fabric, bringing the soul of authentic South Indian vegetarian cooking to every table. Nestled in the heart of Chennai, our kitchen is guided by time-honoured recipes passed down through generations.

From the first crisp of a morning dosa to the warm comfort of a full South Indian meal, every dish we serve carries the essence of home. We use only the freshest ingredients, sourced daily, to ensure each plate is as wholesome as it is flavourful.

Whether you're stopping in for a quick tiffin, a leisurely family lunch, or a satisfying dinner — you'll always leave with a full heart at Hotel Sri Vaari.
